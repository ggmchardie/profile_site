About Me

I am a computer science student/programmer with a background in both library & information services and education. I have worked as a library assistant in the public and private sectors, also as a learning support aide in the public school system. My experiences in these different areas has motivated me to use the skills and opportunities available to me to help others. I hope to build my career based upon this
